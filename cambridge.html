A few suggestions for the project

  • Using vmchecker for automizing the marking of assignments. This system uses virtual machines to test almost any kind of task ranging from simple java programs to checking OS and network configurations. This system is used by the Polytechnic University of Bucharest for evaluating all of their computer science homework assignments.

  • In the case of digital submitted supervision work it would be useful to create a tool, for the supervisor, to let him easily annotate notes to the work(maybe via a table or a laptop with a touch screen). This would enable students to keep all of their supervision work, including important remarks from the supervisor, in a digital format. This would be very useful especially for international students for whom it is impractical to carry large amounts of paper back and forth during vacations. Also students could use this tool to annotate digital course handouts.
